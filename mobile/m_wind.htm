Wind Power Energy Background and Basics

Wind flow in the atmosphere is caused by uneven heating surfaces of the earth, large bodies of water, and the rotation of the earth. Wind energy consists of wind turbines converting the wind flowâ€™s power moving the propellers into usable energy.

Components of the basic wind turbine:

  • □ Nearest to the ground is the mount and the tower. The tower supports the rotor, drivetrain, all of the cables, and anchors the system into the ground.
  • □ The drivetrain contains the gearbox and the generator that stores the energy created from the turbine.
  • □ The rotor or blades of the windmill converts wind energy into rotational shaft energy. This is why the blades rotate in a clockwise or counterclockwise axis.

Benefits of Wind Energy

  • □ Wind power utilization in the United States has TRIPLED in the past ten years
  • □ Wind power is the largest and most effective way of generating renewable energy in the United States
  • □ Houses that live near or at a wind turbine have an increased property value
  • □ The wind power industry has created over 114,000 jobs in the United States
  • □ Overall cost of electricity will decrease

    • ▫ Checking the area of your home with the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and state laws and regulations
