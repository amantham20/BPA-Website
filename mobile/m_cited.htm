Solar Power works by allowing particles of light to knock electrons free from their atoms which generates electricity.

Main Components of a solar panel for a common US house:

  • □ The top layer is an aluminum frame to prevent damage to the panel.
  • □ A glass cover to also prevent harm to the solar panel.
  • □ A panel of stringed solar cells to take in the light particles.
  • □ Then, a junction box to protect the electrical connections leading into the home.
